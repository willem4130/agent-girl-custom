# /headlines - Generate Headline Variations

## What This Does
Creates 10+ headline variations using proven formulas and psychological triggers.

## How to Use
/headlines [topic or product/service]

Example:
/headlines Online course about productivity for remote workers

## Process
1. Analyze the core benefit/transformation
2. Apply 5+ headline formulas
3. Inject psychological triggers
4. Rate each headline for click potential
5. Recommend top 3 with reasoning

## Headline Formulas Applied

### Number Headlines
- "7 Ways to [Achieve Benefit] Without [Common Obstacle]"
- "[Number] [Adjective] [Noun] That Will [Benefit]"

### How-To Headlines
- "How to [Achieve Result] in [Timeframe]"
- "How [Successful Person] [Achieves Result]"

### Question Headlines
- "Are You Making These [Number] [Topic] Mistakes?"
- "What If You Could [Desirable Outcome]?"

### Curiosity Headlines
- "The [Adjective] Secret to [Benefit]"
- "Why [Counterintuitive Statement]"

### Social Proof Headlines
- "[Number] [People] Can't Be Wrong About [Topic]"
- "What [Authority] Knows About [Topic] That You Don't"

## Output Format
1. [Headline] - Formula: [Name], Trigger: [Psychology]
2. [Headline] - Formula: [Name], Trigger: [Psychology]
...

### Top 3 Recommendations
1. **[Best headline]** - Why: [Reasoning]
2. **[Second best]** - Why: [Reasoning]
3. **[Third best]** - Why: [Reasoning]

## Quality Criteria
- Specific over vague
- Benefit-driven
- Curiosity-inducing
- Appropriate length for medium
- Brand voice aligned
