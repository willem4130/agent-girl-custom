# /newsletter - Generate Newsletter Section

## What This Does
Creates compelling newsletter content with strong subject lines and body copy.

## How to Use
/newsletter [topic or brief]

Example:
/newsletter Monthly product update for Q1 2026

## Process
1. Review brand voice profile from session context
2. Generate 3 subject line options (with open rate optimization)
3. Create newsletter body using storytelling framework
4. Include clear CTA variations
5. Self-evaluate against email best practices

## Output Format

### Subject Lines (3 options)
1. [Curiosity-driven option]
2. [Benefit-focused option]
3. [Urgency/FOMO option]

### Preview Text
[The snippet that appears after subject in inbox]

### Newsletter Body
[Main content with proper formatting]

### Call-to-Action Options
1. [Primary CTA]
2. [Alternative CTA]

## Quality Checklist Applied
- Subject line under 50 characters
- Preview text complements (doesn't repeat) subject
- Scannable format with clear hierarchy
- Single primary CTA (not competing actions)
- Brand voice consistent throughout
- No spam trigger words

## Best Practices Embedded
- First line hooks the reader
- Benefits over features
- Social proof where relevant
- Mobile-friendly formatting
- Unsubscribe respect (no guilt-tripping)
